Assaying Black Sands


I also assay Black Sands for gold and silver. The cost is $95.00. I run in triplicate and then take an average. This will give you a good idea of what you might have in a 55gal. barrel. Please take a single representative core sample from your barrel of black sands. I will take three splits from it, fire them, and return the values for each split plus the average. Saves you some money and gives you tighter numbers.
