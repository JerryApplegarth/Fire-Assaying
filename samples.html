Submitting Samples



When submitting a sample, please send about a cup of representative material with an ID(#1, #2, #3, #4, etc) written on a piece of paper in each sample bag. Please do not send multiple pounds of any one sample. I do not need it. I ask for a number in the baggie rather than writing on the baggie because it may smear. Please include your payment, with your name, address, a phone number, and your email address for the results. Please print legibly and double bag if your sample is wet. The Denver USPS sorting facility is notorious for being hard on packages; also please tape your cardboard boxes.

If you call please don't hang up without saying ho or leave a message. I will call you back shortly. Let me know if I can be be of further assistance.
